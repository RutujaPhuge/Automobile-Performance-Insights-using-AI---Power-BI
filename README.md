# Automobile-Performance-Insights-using-AI

  This project Analyzes Atomobile sales, customer behaviour, dealer performance, and vehicle 
  preferences using Power BI. The dashboard helps identify Sales trends, top performing models,
  dealer efficiency, and customer buying patterns.

# Key Insights

  1) Total Sales reached 291M with 10.3K cars sold.
  2) Male customers dominated purchases at nearly 80%.
  3) Quarter-wise sales show consistent growth across the year.
  4) Certain Models and Companies contribute significantly higher Revenue.
  5) SUVs and Hatchbacks show strong customer income association.
  6) Engine type impacts sales volume, with DOHC engines leading.

# KPIs Used
 
  1) Total Cars Sold
  2) Total Sales Amount
  3) Total Number of Dealers
  4) Highest Car Price

# Tools
  Power BI, DAX, Business Insights
